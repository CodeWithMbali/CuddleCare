About Us

Cuddle Care is a trusted health clinic offering comprehensive and confidential healthcare services. In addition to our expertise in maternal and infant care, we provide abortion services, family planning solutions and a variety of health tests. Our team of medical professionals is committed to delivering respectful, high-quality care tailored to each patient’s needs.
